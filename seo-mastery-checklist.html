Phase 1 — Foundations (Weeks 1–3)
Week 1 How Search Engines Work Understand why pages rank
Learn
  • Crawling, indexing, ranking
  • E-E-A-T framework
  • SERP features overview
  • Google's algorithm history
  • Read Search Quality Rater Guidelines (summary)
Practice
  • Search 5 keywords, study top pages
  • Identify SERP features present
  • Set up Google Search Console on a site
  • Explore GSC Performance report
Outcome
  • Can explain why a page ranks
  • Know all major SERP features
  • Understand E-E-A-T concept
  • GSC configured and reading data
Week 2 Keyword Research Fundamentals Build keyword maps
Learn
  • KD vs search volume tradeoff
  • Long-tail discovery methods
  • Keyword clustering techniques
  • CPC as commercial intent signal
  • Competitor gap analysis
Practice
  • Build a 50-keyword list for a niche
  • Cluster into 5–8 topic groups
  • Identify 3 quick-win keywords (low KD)
  • Run a competitor keyword gap in Ahrefs
Outcome
  • Can use Ahrefs/SEMrush for research
  • Understand keyword clustering
  • Can identify opportunity keywords
  • Built a content keyword map
Week 3 Search Intent & Content Matching Match content to searcher goals
Learn
  • 4 intent types in detail
  • SERP analysis for intent signals
  • Content format matching (guide vs list vs comparison)
  • Dominant intent vs mixed intent SERPs
Practice
  • Classify 20 keywords by intent type
  • SERP analysis: what format wins for each?
  • Map 10 keywords to page types
  • Identify 3 keywords with mismatched content
Outcome
  • Can instantly classify search intent
  • Know which content format wins per SERP
  • Can audit pages for intent mismatch

    The 5 skills that handle 80% of agency SEO work. Master these first before moving into advanced topics.
    Keyword Research
    Priority 1

    Every SEO strategy starts here. Without knowing what people search for, you're guessing. Client acquisition, content strategy, and ROI all depend on solid keyword research.

    Ahrefs / SEMrush KD analysis Clustering Intent mapping
    On-Page SEO
    Priority 2

    The most repeatable billable skill. Every page on every client site needs on-page work. Title tags, headings, internal links, and content structure can be applied to hundreds of pages.

    Title tags Content optimization Internal linking Surfer SEO
    Technical Audits
    Priority 3

    A $500–$2,000 deliverable for most agencies. Running a Screaming Frog crawl and presenting findings in a clear report is a fundable skill. Most sites have fixable issues holding them back.

    Screaming Frog GSC Coverage Redirects Core Web Vitals
    Local SEO
    Priority 4

    The single fastest way to show clients measurable results. Local map pack rankings improve within weeks — not months. Every restaurant, tradesperson, and service business is a potential client.

    Google Business Profile Citations Reviews Local schema
    Analytics & Reporting
    Priority 5

    Clients stay when you prove ROI. The difference between a 3-month contract and a long-term retainer is your ability to show month-over-month traffic growth, ranking improvements, and leads from organic search. GA4 + GSC + Looker Studio is the standard stack.

    Google Analytics 4 Search Console Looker Studio Rank tracking Conversion attribution
